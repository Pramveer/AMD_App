MDRD 4-variable GFR Equation

GFR in mL/min per 1.73 m2 = 175 x SerumCr-1.154 x age-0.203 x 1.212 (if patient is black) x 0.742 (if female)

Note: The original Modification of Diet in Renal Disease (MDRD) equation was based on 6 variables: age, sex, ethnicity, serum creatinine, urea, and albumin (often referred to as MDRD 6). Subsequently, the MDR was simplified to a 4-variable version (MDRD-4) that included age, sex, ethnicity, and serum creatinine. This 4-variable version was later reexpressed using serum creatinine values that were standardized to reference methods. The equation shown here is the reexpressed 4-variable MDRD equation.
